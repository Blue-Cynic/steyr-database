Notable Features

Picatinny Rail

The grenade launcher is offered in picatinny rails. This makes it better than the M203, America's old grenade launcher which had to be clamped onto the gun barrel directly, which interferes with the barrel's lifespan and harmonics.

Cold Hammer-forged Barrel

Cold hammer-forged barrel which enhances its lifespan.

Ease of Use

Relatively easy to use thanks to its breech loading and break-action design.
