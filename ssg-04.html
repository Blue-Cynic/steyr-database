Development History

Steyr designed this rifle with as a means to improve upon their older SSG 69. They took many of the SSG 69's features and put it in a much more modern chassis. This rifle would also serve as the basis for their other botlt-action precision rifle, the SSG 08.

The weapon has seen particular success in military and law enforcement agencies.

Notable Features

Safe Bolt System

Uses the Safe Bolt System (SBS) action.

Two-Stage

Like with the Scout, features a unique two-stage magazine system that allows the user to load loose rounds without taking out the magazine on the first stage. The second stage allows the magazine to actually feed the weapon ammunition.

Picatinny Rail

20 MOA Picatinny rail for optics mounting.
