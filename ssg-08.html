Development History

The SSG 08 was ntroduced in 2008, developed with input from Austria's EKO Cobra unit. It is essentially an evolution of the sSG 04 that came out 4 years earlier.

The weapon takes many cues from its predecessos, such as the Safe Bolt System action, and the two-stage magazine system just to name a few. It is offered in more calibers than the SSG 04 and comes with a folding stock for better storage.

Like with the SSG 04, the weapon would find great success in militaries and in law enforcement as a precision sniper rifle.

Notable Features

Folding Stock

Aluminium alloy folding stock with adjustable butt. Makes storing the weapon easier.

Two-Stage Magazine

Allows one to load loose rounds into the magazine without taking it off the weapon via the first stage. The second stage allows the feeding of the ammunition to the gun.

Suppressor Capable

Includes a muzzle break that can host suppressors.
