Welcome to the Steyr Arms Database

Explore the engineering excellence and military heritage of Steyr Arms GmbH, one of Austria's most renowned firearms manufacturers. This database documents their most significant military weapons from the late 20th century to the present day.

Featured Firearms

Steyr AUG

The Steyr AUG (Armee-Universal-Gewehr) is an Austrian bullpup rifle chambered in 5.56×45mm NATO. It is one of the most successful bullpup designs in the world.

Learn More

Steyr Scout

A bolt-action rifle designed alongside Jeff Cooper, a professional shooter and firearms instructor. Designed as a lightweight rifle for increased comfort whilst scouting or exploring outdoors.

Learn More

Steyr M9-A1

A striker-fired semi-automatic polymer pistol notable for its trapezoid sight system. Chambered in 9×19mm Parabellum. Notable for having a low bore axis for a handgun.

About Steyr Arms

Steyr Arms GmbH is an Austrian firearms manufacturer established since 1864. Based in Steyr, Austria, the company has made a name for itself since its founding.

Steyr is committed in the developing and innovating small arms design.
